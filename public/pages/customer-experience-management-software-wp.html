Customer Experience Management Software — FAQ

Customer experience management software is a platform that captures customer feedback across every channel — surveys, reviews, support tickets, social — and turns it into action through AI analytics, journey mapping, and closed-loop workflows. Sogolytics goes further than reporting tools by automatically routing flagged feedback to an owner and tracking it through to resolution.

A Voice of Customer platform aggregates every signal a customer sends — direct feedback, behavioral data, support interactions, and reviews — into one unified view. Sogolytics’ VoC platform adds AI sentiment analysis and automated alerting so teams act on it in real time.

Customer journey analytics maps feedback and behavioral data to specific stages of the customer lifecycle — onboarding, support, renewal, and beyond — to show exactly where experience breaks down, so teams can prioritize fixes at the stage with the highest churn risk.

Closed-loop feedback management means every piece of customer feedback is assigned an owner, tracked to resolution, and reported back to leadership — instead of sitting unread in a survey report.

Survey tools stop at data collection. Sogolytics adds AI-powered sentiment analysis, customer journey analytics, automated workflow routing, and closed-loop case management — so feedback drives a resolved action instead of just a report.
