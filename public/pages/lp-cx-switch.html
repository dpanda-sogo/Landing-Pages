Your Migration Plan

From contract signed to live in 30 days

Our structured migration process has been refined across hundreds of enterprise implementations. Here's exactly what happens.

1
Days 1–3
Discovery & Migration Assessment
Your dedicated implementation manager maps every survey, data connection, user role, and integration in your current environment. You get a detailed migration blueprint before any work begins.
2
Days 4–10
Data Migration & Configuration
We migrate your historical response data, contact lists, and reporting configurations. Your Sogolytics environment is set up in parallel — so your current programs run uninterrupted.

CRM, HRIS, helpdesk, and SSO integrations are wired up and tested. We run a full QA pass — comparing outputs between old and new platforms to verify accuracy before cutover.

Role-based training for your CX team, HR team, and executives. Your team signs off on the environment before we cut over — no surprises at go-live.
